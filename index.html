REAL-TIME FOG REMOVAL SYSTEM AND ITS METHOD THEREOF
The present invention provides a visible and near-infrared band's deep learning and pixel sharing-based system and method for image and video dehazing. It successfully restores hazy/foggy images with a wide range of haze/fog conditions. Unlike the prior art; the proposed invention utilized a wide electromagnetic spectrum for imaging from 400nm to 1400nm wavelength and performed dehazing/ defogging in the entire range (visible and NIR bands).
